Study Debrief

This experiment investigated the "Ecological Valence Theory" of color preferences, based on Palmer & Schloss's (2010) research. This theory suggests that we like colors to the degree that we like the objects and experiences we associate with them.

Your results show your individual color preferences across different dimensions of color (hue, saturation, and lightness). These preferences might reflect your personal experiences and associations with different colors.
